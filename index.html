About me

I'm a UX/UI Designer, front end developer and programming languages explorer.

After more than six years of career I've done many different things, all with the same objective: help people to achieve their business goals by understanding and using technology. I've worked as a graphic designer for web and printed media, full stack developer, systems analyst, infrastructure analyst and project manager. Although very different, each of my experiences helps me to be a better UX professional and for that I face my work as a group of everything I have ever done in my professional life. My bachelor in Information Technology by FIAP also adds many points to that.

I've worked with UX on freelance projects and specialized myself in design by Gama Academy. Nowadays I work as a UX Designer at Conversion Chef, a consulting agency whose goal is to spread the Conversion Rate Optimization practices in Brazil.

I love Internet of Things and EdTechs. My dream is to build fantastic experiences that really contribute to help individuals and groups to have a better education.

My career and studies are guided by the following premises:

  • IoT isn't fiction and UX will have an important role in this field.
  • Algorithms will rule the world and will be the foundation of each time more business models.
  • Know how to code is half way done, the rest are design patterns and developing for the next.
