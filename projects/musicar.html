Musicar

What is it: a mobile application that connects music teachers and students based on their location.

Musicar is a mobile application that makes it easy to connect music students and teachers who're seeking to schedule classes. This project was developed with the help of a multinational music company. To validate the solution, were developed a low fidelity prototype and a landing page to collect the emails from interested people. The solution was presented at Expo Music 2016.

Achievments:
  • Saved money by understanding that the customers wouldn't buy the solution without coding it;
